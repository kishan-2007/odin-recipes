About

Chicken tacos are a traditional Mexican street food staple featuring seasoned, shredded chicken folded into warm tortillas. The dish is customized with various toppings like fresh onions, cilantro, salsa, and lime juice to balance the savory flavor profiles.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Tortillas
  • Diced Onions
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Lime Wedges
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook the chicken breasts until completely cooked through.
  2. Remove the chicken from the skillet and place it on a cutting board.
  3. Use two forks to shred the chicken meat into fine pieces.
  4. Return the shredded chicken to the skillet over low heat.
  5. Add the taco seasoning packet along with a small splash of water to keep the meat moist.
  6. Simmer the mixture for 5 minutes until the chicken fully absorbs the spice blend.
  7. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for 30 seconds on each side.
  8. Place the warm tortillas on a serving platter.
  9. Layer 1: Scoop a generous portion of the seasoned chicken into the center of each tortilla.
  10. Layer 2: Sprinkle a layer of shredded cheese directly over the hot chicken so it melts slightly.
  11. Repeat the process for all remaining tortillas until the chicken filling is fully distributed.
  12. Top Layer: Add the diced onions and freshly chopped cilantro over the cheese layer.
  13. Spoon your preferred salsa evenly across the top layer of the tacos.
  14. Squeeze fresh lime juice from the wedges over the entire filling.
  15. Serve the tacos immediately while the tortillas and meat are still hot.
  16. Resting is not required for this dish; assemble and consume right away.
